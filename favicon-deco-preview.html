MM Mirrored Art-Deco Study

Six dense favicon-sized monogram directions based on the mirrored MM concept. Each keeps the dark tile and pushes the design mass close to the inner edges.

Original

Dense bilateral monogram with a tall central spine and jewel dots.

Stepped

Sharper shoulders and tiered crown geometry for a stricter deco silhouette.

Channel

Extra vertical fluting pushes the mark toward a skyscraper facade.

Fan

Mirrored core with a restrained top fan and a grounded base jewel.

Gate

Portal-like geometry frames the monogram without adding a full border.

Jewel

More explicit diamonds and ornamentation while keeping the mirrored MM dense.
